Location Hoorn


A fresh space full of oxygen provides inspiration and the right focus.
Yoga City is the first public, climate-neutral canal house in Hoorn, the old chocolate factory on the Toneelweg has been lovingly renovated with great love.
For example, we use a super high-tech climate system, the roof is full of solar panels and we have only worked with nature-friendly products to furnish this city oasis.
Not only a conscious design but also one with a soul due to the large amount of antique details and elements with a beautiful story.

Location Alkmaar


In the middle of Alkmaar, but still an oasis of peace.

This is mainly due to the homely interior with many plants, a multifunctional table and a swing on the ceiling as a cheerful note.
