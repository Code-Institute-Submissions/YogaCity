Location Hoorn

The climate-neutral mini church in Hoorn, has been lovingly renovated with great love. For example, we use a super high-tech climate system, the roof is full of solar panels and we have only worked with nature-friendly products to furnish this city oasis. Not only a conscious design but also one with a soul due to the large amount of antique details and elements with a beautiful story. Here you can feel at ease. There are some couches with lots of pillows to relax. At the tea bar you can choose between five fresh herb teas.
