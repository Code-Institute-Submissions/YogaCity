Location Alkmaar


In the middle of Alkmaar, but still an oasis of peace.
In this typical Alkmaar canal house, within walking distance of the Central Station, it is not difficult to let the creativity flow.
This is mainly due to the homely interior with many plants, a multifunctional table and a swing on the ceiling as a cheerful note.
A beautiful glass roof makes you feel you are outdoors.
The bar makes you feel you are in Hawaï, with all the flowers and palm plants. Come and enjoy your little mini holiday with us.
